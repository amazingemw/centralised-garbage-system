CENTRALISED GARBAGE COLLECTOR

THE PROBLEM


In an age of increasing fuel consumption and increasing time restraints, optimization has become an important ideal of the new world. Garbage collection and recycling is a field that optimization should be applied immediately. The amount of garbage output is increasing day by day and garbage bins are filling faster than ever. The usual solution, that of collecting garbage at a given time periodically is going to be vastly insufficient to meet up with the rising demands.
